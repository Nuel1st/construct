The road to success is always under construction

LEBTECH has undertaken assignments on behalf of government agencies, private institutions, financial institutions and public organizations. To our clients, we have always presented quality and speed as the company’s greatest asset, and these have proven to strengthen ties between LEBTECH and its numerous clients. The multi-disciplinary structure of LEBTECH deepens and enriches its specialization in many fields of operation and makes its possible to view projects from a wide range of angles and to combine technological depth with broad vision. The pooling of skills, knowledge and experience of a diversified staff to handle an assignment is particularly relevant in a developing country like Nigeria, where goals and constraints are highly interrelated and do not necessarily all fall within the area or field in which the project is generally classified. The staff at LEBTECH plays active roles in the development of their chosen professions and have made significant contributions, to the technical literature of engineering and economics. They have served as members of the editorial boards of technical journals, on technical committees, and they frequently present papers to technical conferences. To support them, the firm has invested in modern construction equipment's, training facilities in ground modelling and design; structural analysis and design; computer aided design and drafting; economic and financial modelling; acoustical modelling and analysis; power-system analysis; geotechnical analysis; highway-lighting design; traffic modelling and statistical analysis. The computer services support group provides an active software development capability supporting the entire construction department. .
